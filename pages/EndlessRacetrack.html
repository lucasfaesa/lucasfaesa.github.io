Endless [Music] Racetrack

Endless (Music) Racer was a personal project aimed at gaining more insight into procedural environment generation. It involves creating a game where the player endlessly drives their car, navigating ramps, with no significant gameplay beyond that.

Another goal was to have the scenery react to the beats of the music. However, when the game was exported to WebGL, this feature partially ceased to function, causing the colors of the environment to randomly flicker.
