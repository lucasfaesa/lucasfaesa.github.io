Third Bridge

“Third Bridge” was created as a challenge/learning experience; I wanted to delve into shaders and a bit of modeling. However, in this game, I only modeled the bridge and its lamp posts; the rest consisted of assets easily found on Sketchfab. The shaders I used included effects of droplets falling to the ground and droplets sliding along the mesh of objects, as well as a water shader.

In terms of gameplay, there's nothing particularly unique; the character simply walks/crouches, and the player must follow a fairly linear path.
