Remember Us

"Remember Us" was a game created for a game jam at the college where I was studying. It heavily relies on emotional aspects, and its gameplay is essentially about looking around and clicking on items.

It was interesting to learn about rendering 3D objects on the canvas, and it sparked my curiosity about optimization, especially since the game was intended to be played in the browser and featured many graphically intensive assets.
